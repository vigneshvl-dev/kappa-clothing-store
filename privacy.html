Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 18, 2026

At KAPPA Fashion Store, accessible from our storefront and pages, one of our main priorities is the privacy of our visitors. This Privacy Policy document contains types of information that is collected and recorded by KAPPA and how we use it.

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal information that you provide to us when you make purchases, create accounts, or contact us. This includes your name, shipping address, email address, phone number, and billing parameters. Additionally, our payment systems capture mock token representations for online payments, keeping your real credentials entirely secure.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect in various ways, including to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain our e-commerce platform.
  • Improve, personalize, and expand our clothing offerings.
  • Understand and analyze how you interact with our pages.
  • Process transactions, fulfill shipping requests, and send order notifications.
  • Send customer support communications.

3. Log Files & Analytics

KAPPA follows a standard procedure of using log files. These files log visitors when they visit websites. The information collected by log files includes internet protocol (IP) addresses, browser type, Internet Service Provider (ISP), date and time stamp, referring/exit pages, and number of clicks. These are not linked to any information that is personally identifiable.

4. Cookies & Web Beacons

Like any other website, KAPPA uses 'cookies'. These cookies are used to store information including visitors' preferences, shopping cart history, and the pages on the website that the visitor accessed. The information is used to optimize the users' experience by personalizing our web page content.

5. Consent

By using our website, you hereby consent to our Privacy Policy and agree to its terms and conditions.
